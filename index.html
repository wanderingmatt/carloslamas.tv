Bio

Carlos Lamas is a freelance video editor from Minneapolis, working in post-production since 2005. He has edited for countless national brands including Target, Estée Lauder, Facebook, Kohl's, ESPN, and Best Buy. He has also edited several feature films and music videos. When he's not working, Carlos is either at the beach or skiing, depending on the season.

Carlos was raised bilingual, and loves to edit in Spanish.
